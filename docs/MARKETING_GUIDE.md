## 🚀 Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Weeks 1-2)

### Influencer Partnerships

#### Finding the Right Influencers

**Target Micro-Influencers** (10k-100k followers):

- More engagement than mega-influencers
- Better ROI for budget travel
- More authentic with audience

**Categories to Target:**

1. **Budget Travel Influencers**
   - Search: #BudgetTravelIndia, #BackpackerIndia
   - Instagram: @travelwithgoa, @backpackpacker_india
2. **Adventure Influencers**
   - Hiking & trekking content creators
   - Instagram search: #TrekGuru, #AdventureIndia
3. **Food & Travel**
   - Food bloggers who travel
   - Instagram search: #FoodieTravel

4. **Lifestyle/Digital Nomads**
   - Working while traveling
   - Instagram search: #DigitalNomadIndia

#### Outreach Strategy

**Email Template:**

```
Subject: Collab Opportunity - Budget Travel Platform [Your Name]

Hi [Name],

I love your travel content, especially [mention specific post].

We're launching MOMENTRY - India's first budget travel platform
designed for backpackers like your audience.

We'd like to offer:
✈️ Free trip for you + 1 friend (any of our packages)
💰 Commission: 10-15% on every booking from your link
📸 Exclusive content creation opportunity

Your audience gets: Exclusive discount code (INFLUENCER15)

Let's chat!
Best,
[Your Name]
```

#### First 10 Influencers Target

1. **Tier 1**: 50k-100k followers (3-5 influencers)
   - Offer: Free trip + 15% commission
2. **Tier 2**: 20k-50k followers (5-7 influencers)
   - Offer: Free trip + 12% commission

3. **Tier 3**: 10k-20k followers (10+ influencers)
   - Offer: Free trip + 10% commission

**Expected ROI:** 1 influencer with 50k followers can bring 500+ bookings if content is good.
